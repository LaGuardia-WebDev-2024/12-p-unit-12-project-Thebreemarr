Memory #1: Beach

I went to the beach so many times over the last year and loved every second of it. My favorite days that I spent at the beach were:
Apr 29, 2024 This was our spring break in 2024, and my first time back at the
beach since the winter- My brother and I went in the ocean with
full wetsuits on because of how cold it was. It was totally worth it.
May 26, 2024 An amazing day I spent with my friends hanging out by the beach
eating popcorn, tanning, and swimming in the perfect ocean.
Jun 16, 2024 I spent my regents week (when I wasnt testing) living it up by
the beach with my family and best friends. We mini golfed, soaked
in the hot tub, went on bikerides, watched the sunsets, surfed,
and went on an epic boat ride to the lighthouse!
Sep 22, 2024 My favorite season to be at the beach has always been the
fall; when the crowds leave and the water is warmer than
the air. This was a perfect day to swim in the ocean and
I was blessed with my friends being there with me.

Memory #2: Camping

Every year, my family goes camping on Labor Day weekend. I love being out in nature. the Pocono Mountains are a beautiful place to be. We always find animals like the Eastern newt, frogs, and fluffy caterpillars on our hikes. We do super fun activities like kayaking, swimming in the Delaware River, tie dying, bird watching, photography, swimming in the lake, and singing by the campfire.

Memory #3: Acting

This year I have done 3 scenes in acting class and 2 shakespeare shows over the summer. I've had fun being an actor this year- its a main aspect of my life.
